About

I'm Lucila Scherr an Argentinian UX Engineer exploring and living in Sydney, Australia. Frontend engineer with a design background who loves solving challenging problems. I’m specialized in the full interaction that users have with software, from its design to its development, as well as usability, performance, and accessibility.
Previously, I've moved between design and development in an innovative Argentinian startup and a tech company.

I love connecting with and understanding people. I get jazzed leading in-depth user research and brainstorming solutions. Collaboration and teamwork are my jam.
